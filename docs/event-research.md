# Event Research

Event Research is Polytragent's flagship feature. It runs a comprehensive AI analysis on any Polymarket event and delivers actionable insights in seconds.

## How to Use

**Method 1: From the menu**
1. Open `/menu`
2. Tap **🔬 Event Research**
3. Paste a Polymarket event link when prompted

**Method 2: Auto-detect (recommended)**
Simply paste any Polymarket link directly in the chat — the bot detects it automatically and starts researching.

```
https://polymarket.com/event/will-bitcoin-hit-100k
```

## What You Get

The AI analysis includes:

### Market Analysis
- Current YES/NO prices
- 24h trading volume
- Liquidity depth
- Market deadline

### AI Recommendation
- Buy YES, Buy NO, or SKIP
- Confidence level (Low/Medium/High)
- Reasoning based on 5-source weighted analysis

### Edge Detection
- Expert forecaster comparison (Metaculus, Swift Centre)
- Market vs expert pricing divergence
- Overpricing factor calculation

### Whale Activity
- Smart money flow direction
- Top wallet positions on this market
- Conviction signals

### News Context
- Recent developments affecting the event
- Catalyst timeline
- Risk factors

### Entry/Exit Strategy
- Recommended entry price range
- Take profit target
- Stop-loss level
- Position sizing suggestion

## After Research

Once analysis is complete, you'll see action buttons:

- **🔬 Research Event** — Analyze another event
- **📊 Portfolio** — View your positions
- **💰 Trade** — Go to trading strategies
- **← Main Menu** — Return to main menu

## Tips

- Research works best on binary YES/NO markets with clear deadlines
- Events with higher volume produce more reliable whale data
- The AI cross-references Polymarket pricing with Kalshi when available
- Research takes approximately 30-60 seconds for a full analysis
