FAQ

Is my wallet safe?

Yes. Only your public address is stored. No private keys, no signing, no custody. Same as viewing your wallet on Polygonscan.

Can Polytragent trade for me?

No. Polytragent provides research, signals, and alerts. You execute trades on Polymarket directly.

How do Whale Alerts work?

Follow wallets from the Monthly Leaderboard or add addresses manually. When a followed wallet trades, you get an instant Telegram notification.

Are strategies guaranteed to profit?

No. All trading involves risk. Strategies are based on quantitative analysis and historical patterns. Never trade more than you can afford to lose.

How do I cancel?

Send /manage in the bot to get your Stripe subscription management link. Cancel anytime.

The bot isn't responding

Wait 30 seconds and try again. If it persists, the bot may be restarting. Try /menu after a few minutes.
