# Getting Started

## Creating Your Account

1. Open Telegram on your phone or desktop
2. Search for **@bmpolybot** or click the bot link shared with you
3. Tap **Start** or send `/menu`
4. The bot will greet you and show subscription options

Your account is created automatically when you first interact with the bot. Your Telegram username and chat ID are stored securely.

### How to Subscribe

1. Send `/subscribe` or tap **Subscribe — $79.99/mo** in the bot
2. You'll receive a secure Stripe payment link
3. Complete payment with your credit card
4. Access is activated instantly
5. Subscription renews monthly — cancel anytime from Settings

## Access Codes

If you have an access code (format: `PTA-XXXXXXXX`), you can activate your account without a credit card:

1. Send `/code` in the bot
2. Enter your access code when prompted
3. Or use directly: `/code PTA-XXXXXXXX`

Access codes are provided by Polytragent team members and partners. Each code can only be used once.

## First Steps After Activation

Once subscribed, we recommend:

1. **Connect your wallet** — Go to Settings and paste your public Polymarket wallet address to see live positions and P&L
2. **Try Event Research** — Paste any Polymarket event link in the chat to get instant AI analysis
3. **Set up Whale Alerts** — Follow top traders to get notifications when they make moves
4. **Explore Strategies** — Check out NO Theta and Scalping signals for AI-guided trading
